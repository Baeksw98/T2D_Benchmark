# Data Use Terms

This file governs the **data** in this repository (the synthetic vignettes under
`data/vignettes/`, the G-theory estimates under `data/g_theory/`, and the
synthetic demo tables under `data/demo/`). The **code** is licensed separately
under the MIT License (see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE)).

## Grant

The released data are made available for academic, non-commercial research and
for independent verification of the benchmark's methodology and measurement
properties. You may use, copy, and redistribute the released data for these
purposes, provided that you cite the paper (see [`CITATION.cff`](CITATION.cff))
and retain this notice.

## Nature of the data

- The vignettes are **fully synthetic**. They are not derived from, and do not
  correspond to, any real patient. They must not be used as if they were real
  clinical data.
- The G-theory CSVs are **aggregate variance-component estimates**. They contain
  no per-patient scores, rater identities, or rubric text.
- The demo tables are **artificial** and are not the paper's results.

## Not included

The patient-specific gold-standard rubric content, the production CDSS prompt
templates, and the raw AI rater outputs are **not** part of this release.
Reasonable requests for these materials, for academic, non-commercial research,
are reviewed by the corresponding authors on a case-by-case basis and may be
granted under a data use agreement.

## No clinical use

The materials are for research and evaluation of AI systems. They are **not**
medical advice and must not be used to guide the care of any real patient.
